Finition is a calligraphic brush script. 
Created by Måns Grebäck, this typeface is perfect to create a youthful logo. 
With its hundreds of glyphs it supports a wide range of languages. 

Use underscore to make an underline. 
Example: Fin_ition

With ligatures activated, write multiple underscores for a longer line. 
Example: Aut___omatic
